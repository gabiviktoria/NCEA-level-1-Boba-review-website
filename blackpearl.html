Review

My friends and I ordered from Black Pearl on uber eats after a badminton tournament. Besides bubble tea they actuallt sell a wide range of asian dishes. The Black Pearl bubble tea menu has a lot of drink and topping choices. My order was a regular sized matcha milk tea with pearls, no ice and 70% sweetness. Our bubble tea order took 40 minutes to arrive which was a bit disappointing but at least the delivery was free.

I really like the unique shape of their cup. It was unfortumtely plastic as was the seel and the straw. They also have their logo on the seal like Gong Cha but their cups are a bit wider.

Since the sweetness level was 70%, the first sip was overwhelmingly sweet for me. After a while I got used to it but it still wasn’t preferable. The sweetness definitely overpowered the matcha taste but at least I could still taste that it was there. Overall I liked the drink but I almost couldn't taste he matcha over the sweetness.

The pearls were perfectly chewy and almost the idea size. They were bigger than the Gong Cha so they got stuck in the straw once and it was hard to get unstuck. There was a bigger pearl to drink ratio than Gong Cha and Noah's Ark which I was obviously happy with.
